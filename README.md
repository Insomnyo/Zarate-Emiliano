# Proyecto: Guía Esencial de D&D para Principiantes

## 3. Consignas Prácticas

### 3.1 Definición del Público Objetivo
Para el desarrollo de este proyecto web, se ha definido el siguiente perfil de usuario principal:

- Rango etario: Predominantemente jóvenes y adultos a partir de los 15 años (15+).
- Nacionalidad: Usuarios de habla hispana, con foco en Latinoamérica, debido a la falta de recursos simplificados en español.
- Ubicación geográfica: Contexto urbano con acceso a internet.
- Variables adicionales relevantes: 
    - Nivel educativo: Estudiantes y profesionales interesados en juegos de rol.
    - Alfabetización digital: Media; usuarios que consumen contenido en plataformas digitales y redes sociales.
    - Dispositivos de acceso: Smartphones para consulta rápida durante la partida y tablets/PC para lectura profunda.
    - Idioma principal: Español (con glosario de términos técnicos).

---

### 3.2 Registro de Inspiraciones y Proceso Creativo (Creative Process)

#### 1. Design inspirations
- Classic Fantasy Literature: The visual identity uses elements from medieval manuscripts and ancient scrolls, prioritizing a dark theme to evoke an epic and mysterious atmosphere.
- D&D Beyond & RPG Video Games: I analyzed official tools and modern RPG HUDs (Heads-Up Displays) to create an interface that feels familiar to gamers but remains intuitive for absolute beginners.
- Dark Fantasy Aesthetic: The project is inspired by the immersive feeling of exploring a magical tome, focusing on high-contrast elements for better readability.

#### 2. Creative process documentation
The core challenge was organizing a vast amount of data (Combat, DM tips, Roleplay, and Rules) without overwhelming the user.

- Decisions made: I structured the content into six main pillars: Races & Classes, Dice & Abilities, Character Stats, Combat Flow, General Rules, and Roleplaying basics. I also included a dedicated Dungeon Master (DM) section. To differentiate from D&D Beyond's red/black scheme, I chose a deep purple and black palette to give the guide a unique identity that suggests ancient magic and arcane lore.
- Discarded alternatives: I initially thought about a single-page scrolling layout, but I discarded it in favor of a categorized navigation menu to help users find specific rules (like "Combat turns") instantly during a live session.
- Evolution: The project evolved from a basic glossary to a comprehensive "Quick-Start Guide." I prioritized high-contrast typography to ensure readability of complex rules like combat mechanics and ability checks.
